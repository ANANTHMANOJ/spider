About Us

The 36thChamber is a CA coaching center started with the intention to guide and help CA students to achieve their goal of becoming an Chartered Accountant.

We Constantly work and strive for innvative ways of teaching and assessment.

We firmly believe in the power of Technology and look for ways to incorporate the same in our training courses.

What we do

We are Committed to provide the best coaching to CA students and get the best out of them. We also provide SAP training to Chartered Accountants and other Finance Professionals.
